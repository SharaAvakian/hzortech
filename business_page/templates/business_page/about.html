About HzorTech

HzorTech was built from the expertise of Shara Avakian, a cybersecurity and cloud engineer with hands-on experience building secure, enterprise-grade systems for banking, high-performance compute research, and global IT support.

Our Mission

Deliver practical, secure technology services that reduce risk, automate operations, and support growth for small and medium enterprises.

Our Vision

Become a trusted partner for clients who need cloud engineering, cybersecurity, and resilient operations, drawing from real-world finance and research infrastructure experience.

Founders & Early Identity

Shara Avakian founded the company after roles as a junior cloud engineer at a national research institute, a security and QA specialist at Senex Security, and technical support for banking workflow systems at Golden SB-Tech. HzorTech is intentionally lean, agile, and customer-focused.

Company Values

  • Integrity in security and data handling
  • Timely delivery with measurable impact
  • Data-backed decisions and continuous improvement
  • Collaboration and transparent communication

Team Expertise

Our team specializes in DevOps, cloud automation (Python, Bash, PowerShell), Blue Team cybersecurity (vulnerability management, incident response), and systems integration for Windows, Linux, and container environments.

Client-first mindset

We work alongside your developers and operators, maintaining service reliability while transferring knowledge through workshops, documentation, and hand-over sessions.

Practical Example Engagement

At Golden SB-Tech, we monitored payment workflows and resolved production issues for banking services in real time. At Senex Security, we reduced high-risk vulnerabilities on more than 30,000 endpoints and supported key clients like Ford Saudi Arabia and Bank of Dubai.
